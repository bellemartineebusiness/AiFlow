Last updated: 22 March 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how AiFlow / TikTok Money Machine ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

In accordance with the EU ePrivacy Directive (Directive 2002/58/EC) and the GDPR, we require your informed consent before placing any non-essential cookies on your device.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Similar technologies include local storage (used to save preferences in your browser without expiry), session storage (cleared when you close your browser tab), and tracking pixels.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential / Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function. They do not require your consent under EU law.

  • cookie_consent (local storage)
    Purpose: Stores your cookie consent preference (accepted or declined) so you are not shown the cookie banner on every visit.
    Duration: Persistent (stored in your browser's local storage until you clear it).
    Provider: AiFlow (first-party).

Analytics Cookies (requires consent)

We may use analytics tools in the future to understand how visitors use our site. These cookies will only be set if you click "Accept All" on our cookie banner.

Currently, we do not use any third-party analytics or advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and request your consent before placing such cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

When you use the AI assistant, your messages are sent to OpenAI's API. This is a server-to-server call and does not involve cookies being placed on your device by OpenAI.

We do not use any social media tracking pixels, advertising networks, or remarketing cookies.

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not use pre-ticked consent boxes.
  • We do not place non-essential tracking cookies before you give consent.
  • We do not use cookie walls (forcing you to accept cookies to use the site).
  • We do not sell data collected via cookies to third parties.

4. Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit our site, a cookie banner will appear allowing you to:

  • Accept All — consent to all cookies including any future analytics cookies.
  • Decline — only essential cookies (listed above) will be used.

Change or Withdraw Your Consent

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage. In most browsers you can do this via:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files / Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search and services → Clear browsing data

After clearing, the cookie banner will appear again on your next visit and you can make a new choice.

Browser-Level Cookie Controls

You can also control cookies directly through your browser settings. Note that disabling all cookies may affect website functionality. For more information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add new features or technologies that involve cookies. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. We will re-request consent if we introduce new non-essential cookies.
