Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 22 March 2025

This Refund and Cancellation Policy describes your rights regarding refunds and cancellations when using services provided by AiFlow / TikTok Money Machine. Your rights as a European Union consumer are protected under the EU Consumer Rights Directive (2011/83/EU) and applicable Swedish consumer law.

1. Free Services

The educational content and AI assistant currently available on this website are provided free of charge. No payment is required to access them, and therefore no refund policy applies to the free service.

2. Right of Withdrawal (EU Consumers) — 14-Day Cooling-Off Period

If you are a consumer based in the European Union or European Economic Area and you purchase a paid digital service or subscription from us, you have the right to withdraw from your purchase within 14 calendar days from the date of purchase, without giving any reason.

This right is provided under the EU Consumer Rights Directive.

How to Exercise Your Right of Withdrawal

To withdraw from a purchase, send a clear written statement to us within 14 days of purchase. You can use the model withdrawal form below or simply send an email stating your intention to withdraw.

Contact for withdrawal requests:
📧 contact@aiflow.se
Subject: Withdrawal from purchase — [your order reference or email address]

You may (but are not required to) use the following form:

3. Exceptions to the Right of Withdrawal

In accordance with Art. 16(m) of the EU Consumer Rights Directive, the right of withdrawal may not apply to digital content (such as downloadable files) if:

  • The digital content has already been delivered, and
  • You gave your prior explicit consent to begin delivery before the withdrawal period expired, and
  • You acknowledged that you thereby lose your right of withdrawal.

We will clearly inform you of these conditions and request your explicit consent before beginning delivery of any digital content that would be exempt from withdrawal.

For subscriptions, you retain the right of withdrawal for the full 14 days from the date of the initial subscription purchase.

4. Refund Process

If your withdrawal request is valid, we will:

  • Confirm receipt of your withdrawal request by email.
  • Process your refund within 14 calendar days of receiving your withdrawal notice.
  • Issue the refund using the same payment method you used for the original purchase, unless you have explicitly agreed otherwise.
  • Refund the full amount paid, including any standard delivery costs (if applicable).

5. Cancellation of Subscriptions

If paid subscription features are offered, you may cancel your subscription at any time:

  • By email: contact@aiflow.se with subject "Cancel subscription"
  • Through your account settings (when available)

Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will continue to have access to the service until the end of the paid period. We do not offer partial refunds for unused subscription time unless required by law or the 14-day right of withdrawal applies.

We will send a confirmation email when your cancellation has been processed.

6. Trial Periods

If we offer a free trial period for a paid subscription, we will clearly state:

  • The duration of the trial.
  • What happens at the end of the trial (i.e., whether payment begins automatically).
  • How to cancel before the trial converts to a paid subscription.

You will not be charged without clear and explicit notice before any billing occurs.

If you have questions about refunds, cancellations, or your consumer rights, please contact us:

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

This policy does not affect your statutory rights as a consumer. For further information about your consumer rights in Sweden or the EU, you can visit:
