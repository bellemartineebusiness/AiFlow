Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how AiFlow / TikTok Money Machine ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores and protects personal data when you visit our website or use our services, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Swedish law.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

  • Company: AiFlow
  • Email: contact@aiflow.se

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please contact us at the email address above.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

Automatically collected data

  • IP address — collected automatically by our web server when you visit the site.
  • Browser and device information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language settings.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on the site, and referring URLs.
  • Server log files — technical logs for security and performance purposes.

Data you provide voluntarily

  • Messages sent to the AI assistant — the content of questions and chat messages you submit. These are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses.
  • Email address — if you contact us directly by email or sign up for a future subscription or mailing list.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — for server logs, IP addresses, and security purposes.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — for analytics cookies, if you accept them via our cookie banner.
  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) — for data needed to deliver a paid service or subscription.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — when we are required by law to retain certain records.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver and operate the website and AI assistant service.
  • To process and respond to your AI chat queries.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive use of the service.
  • To improve the website and user experience (with your consent for analytics).
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

5. Third Parties We Share Data With

We share your data with the following third parties only as necessary to deliver our service:

  • OpenAI (openai.com) — your AI chat messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses. OpenAI processes data under its own Privacy Policy. We send only the content of your message; we do not send identifying information.
  • Hosting provider — our web server provider has access to server logs including IP addresses. They act as a data processor under a Data Processing Agreement.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

6. International Data Transfers

OpenAI is based in the United States. When your AI chat messages are processed by OpenAI, your data may be transferred to and processed in the USA. OpenAI participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and implements Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure adequate data protection.

7. How Long We Retain Your Data

  • Server logs (IP addresses, access logs) — retained for a maximum of 90 days, then automatically deleted.
  • AI chat messages — we do not store the content of chat messages on our servers beyond the duration of your browser session. OpenAI may retain data per its own retention policy.
  • Account data (if applicable) — retained for as long as your account is active, or up to 2 years after account closure.
  • Legal obligation records — retained for as long as required by applicable law (typically 7 years for financial records in Sweden).

8. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on this site. Please see our Cookie Policy for full details. You can manage your cookie preferences through the cookie banner displayed on your first visit.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure / "right to be forgotten" (Art. 17) — you can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18) — you can ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3)) — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting previous processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@aiflow.se. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe we have not handled your personal data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish data protection authority:

  • Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY)
    Website: www.imy.se
    Email: imy@imy.se

11. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. These include HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews. We collect only the data necessary for the purposes described above (data minimisation).

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.
